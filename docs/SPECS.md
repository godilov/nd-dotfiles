## Key-scheme

### Meta

Keys:  
- h: move left  | decrease | close
- j: move down  | decrease | close
- k: move up    | increase | open
- l: move right | increase | open

Modifiers:  
- Super: Desktop
- Shift: Move
- Ctrl: Move to Screen
- Alt: Move to Space
